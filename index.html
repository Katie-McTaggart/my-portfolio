About Me

I'm a highly innovative person with experience in renewable energy, software, and electrical engineering. I have a Bachelor's of Engineering in Sustainable and Renewable Energy Engineering.

While I can function in many different work environments, I especially thrive in jobs that highlight innovation and collaborative work, such as my most recent company where I was an Engineering Intern in charge of the monitoring system of a dewatering company, WJ Groundwater Canada Ltd. In that job, for example, after discussions with my team to find out what they needed, I engineered an entirely new alarm system using Javascript to alert project managers to issues on their sites.

After that, I began a Project Management position where I collaborated with the design engineers, on-site well technicians, and the client to ensure client satisfaction with project progress or completion.

I then returned to my previous role part-time through my last year of university to ensure the code continued to function effectively, as they found my software to be very useful.
